Safety Guidelines

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Your safety is our top priority. Cruzr connects you with other travelers on your cruise, but we want to ensure you have a safe and positive experience. Please read these safety guidelines carefully.

üõ°Ô∏è General Safety Tips

1. Meet in Public Places

  • Always meet in public areas of the cruise ship (e.g., restaurants, bars, pool deck)
  • Avoid meeting in private cabins, especially on first meetings
  • Choose well-lit, populated areas

2. Tell Someone Your Plans

  • Inform a friend or family member when and where you're meeting someone
  • Share the person's profile or name
  • Check in with someone during or after your meeting

3. Keep Personal Information Private

  • Do not share your cabin number until you feel comfortable
  • Avoid sharing financial information (bank accounts, credit cards)
  • Be cautious about sharing your home address or workplace
  • Do not share sensitive personal details too quickly

4. Trust Your Instincts

  • If something feels wrong, trust your gut and leave
  • You are never obligated to continue a conversation or meeting
  • Don't feel pressured to do anything you're uncomfortable with

5. Stay Sober and Alert

  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption when meeting someone new
  • Never leave your drink unattended
  • Be aware of your surroundings at all times

6. Do Not Send Money

  • Never send money to someone you've only met online
  • Be wary of sob stories or urgent financial requests
  • Report any users who ask for money
