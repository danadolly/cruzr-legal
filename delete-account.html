Cruzr — Delete Account

This page explains how to delete your Cruzr account and associated data.

Delete your account inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Cruzr
  2. Go to Me → Settings
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm

If deletion requires re-authentication, sign out and sign back in, then try again.

Delete your account if you can’t access the app

Email us from the email address associated with your Cruzr account: cruzrapp@outlook.com

Subject line suggestion: Cruzr Account Deletion Request

Deletion timeline & retention

  • We aim to delete or de-identify profile information within 30 days of your deletion request.
  • Some information may be retained longer where needed for safety, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal compliance (for example, records of reports/abuse).
