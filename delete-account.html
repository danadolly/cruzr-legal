How to Delete Your Cruzr Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Cruzr account and personal data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete Within the App

To delete your account directly from the Cruzr mobile app:

  1. Open the Cruzr app
  2. Go to Settings (tap your profile icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Account"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Email Us

What Happens After Deletion?

Once you request account deletion:

  • Your profile will be immediately hidden from other users
  • Your matches and conversations will be deleted
  • We aim to delete or de-identify your personal data within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained longer if required by law, for fraud prevention, safety investigations, or dispute resolution

Data Retention

After deletion, we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Transaction records (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Records of reported safety incidents
  • Anonymized analytics data (which cannot identify you)
