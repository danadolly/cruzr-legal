Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your profile information, matches, messages, and photos will be deleted.

Delete your account inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Cruzr app
  2. Go to Me ‚Üí Settings
  3. Scroll down and select Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision

Note: If deletion requires re-authentication, sign out and sign back in, then try the deletion process again.

Delete your account if you can't access the app

If you're unable to access the Cruzr app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email from the email address associated with your Cruzr account to: [email protected]
  2. Use the subject line: "Cruzr Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your full name and the email address used for your account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your deletion request

What happens after deletion?

Once you delete your account:

  • Immediate: Your profile will be hidden from other users immediately
  • Within 90 days: We will delete or de-identify your profile information, photos, matches, and messages
  • Exceptions: Some information may be retained longer where needed for:
    • Safety and fraud prevention
    • Dispute resolution
    • Legal compliance and investigations
    • Records of reports or Terms of Service violations

Data retention timeline

  • Profile data: Deleted within 90 days
  • Photos: Deleted within 90 days
  • Messages: Deleted within 90 days
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Safety/abuse reports: Retained as needed for safety and legal compliance

Alternative: Temporarily hide your profile

If you're not sure about permanently deleting your account, consider temporarily hiding your profile instead:

  1. Go to Me ‚Üí Settings
  2. Toggle "Hide My Profile"
  3. You can reactivate anytime by toggling it back
