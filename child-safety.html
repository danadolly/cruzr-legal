Cruzr - Child Safety Standards

Age Restriction

Cruzr is strictly for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly permit users under 18 to create accounts or use our service.

Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Prevention

Cruzr has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and child exploitation. We prohibit:

  • Any content depicting minors in sexual or suggestive contexts
  • Solicitation or grooming of minors
  • Distribution of child sexual abuse material
  • Any behavior that endangers children

Detection and Reporting

We employ multiple safeguards:

  • Age verification: Users must confirm they are 18+ during registration
  • In-app reporting: Users can report inappropriate content or behavior
  • Manual review: Reported content is reviewed by our team
  • Account suspension: Violators are immediately banned
  • Law enforcement cooperation: We report CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local authorities

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter child safety concerns in Cruzr:

  • In-app: Use the "Report" button on any profile or message
  • Email: cruzrapp@outlook.com
  • Emergency: Contact local law enforcement immediately
